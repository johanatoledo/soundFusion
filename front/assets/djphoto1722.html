    The Best Sound in the Business

    With over 20 years of industry experience, we've worked with established and up-and-coming clients and for events of all sizes. Whether we are working on large concert venues, club and bar sound, or providing DJ services, we're passionate about delivering incredible sound. Our years of experience and high-tech equipment, not to mention our artistic vision, ensure that Sound Fusion can always deliver. Get in touch today to find out what Sound Fusion Entertainment can do for you.

    About

    Our owner has over 20 years of experience in the field of Sound Engineering, and has worked in Baltimore City clubs.
    The owner has trained all staff above production standard. All of our staff love working with sound equipment and production lighting, and specialize in knowing how to tune it to create the best listening and lighting experience for our customers. Our equipment is of the highest quality and receives regular maintenance. Our lighting is of the newest variety and technology.
    We have receive lots and lots of kudos from previous clients in regards to our customer service. We strive to provide you with a custom entertainment schedule for your event. Each event comes with a complementary sit-down consultation, so that we can be sure you will love our service.
